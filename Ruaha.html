Welcome to Ruaha National Park

Ruaha National Park is one of Tanzania's largest and most pristine national parks, located in the southern part of the country.

Ruaha National Park, It covers an area of about 20,226 square kilometers (7,809 square miles), making it one of the largest national parks in Tanzania and East Africa as a whole. The park is situated within the Iringa region

It's renowned for its diverse wildlife, including
  • elephants
  • lions
  • leopards
  • giraffes
  • hippos.
    • The Great Ruaha River flows through the park, providing a lifeline for the animals, especially during the dry season when the river becomes a focal point for wildlife viewing. Ruaha offers fantastic opportunities for safaris, birdwatching, and experiencing the natural beauty of East Africa.
